Introduction

It’s been almost seven years since a Hollow Knight DLC became one of the most anticipated sequels in the last 10 years.

After beating the base game, i would define Silksong as team cherry reflecting how much experience they’ve gained after hollow knight success.

first time playing and enjoying and big videogame full of secrets Silksong is a game that reflects how much experience team cherry gain with hollow knight History is most comprehensive, they really know what they want to tell us. Sections transitions are better

First impressions

The first few hours of the game reveals one thing: it’s boxed in the concept of the first game, not due to its Metroidvania structure, but because Hollow Knight is its —i’d argue— own genre. For this reason, one of the biggest challenge for TC was recreate and -ironically- innovate their insight mechanics.

In hollow knight, there’s elements that already works perfectly, so instead team cherry had a challenge about how to manage or implement these ideas without breaking the nature of the game: sharka vs cornifer bellbeast vs the laststag diagonal vs horizontal pogo flees vs grubs
